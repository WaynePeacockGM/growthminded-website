Background

The Inside Track to Executive Success

Former IBM Sector Lead, Global Board Assessor, and Strategic Advisor.

Wayne Peacock is a coach, advisor and speaker with more than 30 years of experience in digital leadership and transformation. His career spans some of the world's most complex organisations, where he has led teams, built capability, and created the conditions for people and businesses to thrive.

As a former IBM Executive, Wayne served as a Sector Lead running a major commercial business unit within the Financial Services market, working with global banks and financial institutions to steer high-stakes, transformational change. He also served on IBM's Global Assessment Board, evaluating and assessing candidates for Executive promotion. This gives him a rare, inside view of what organisations truly look for at the highest level. He brings that breadth of experience directly into his coaching practice: pragmatic, evidence-based, and deeply focused on impact.

Wayne is a sought-after keynote workshop speaker, and his signature Stick Figure Framework has helped hundreds of professionals build a complete picture of their professional identity, from their technical craft to their leadership evidence and personal narrative.

A Passion for Developing People

Wayne's coaching is rooted in a genuine belief that every professional has more to offer than they realise, and that the right support can unlock that potential.

Building High-Performing Teams

Having led major programmes at a global technology firm, Wayne understands what it takes to build and sustain teams that perform under pressure and deliver lasting results.

Strategic Problem-Solving

Wayne brings structured, strategic thinking to every coaching engagement, helping clients cut through complexity and focus on what will move the needle.

Diversity & Inclusion

Wayne serves on the LEAD Network advisory board for the Digital Chapter and was recognised as Volunteer of the Year 2024.
