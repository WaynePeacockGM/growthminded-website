FAQ

Common questions

How long are coaching programmes?

Most individual coaching programmes run over 3–6 months with regular sessions. We'll discuss the right structure on your intro call.

Are sessions online or in-person?

Both. Most clients choose video sessions for flexibility, but in-person sessions in London can be arranged for those who prefer it.

Who is coaching for?

Individual coaching is for ambitious professionals at any level, from senior ICs building a case for promotion to leaders wanting to sharpen their narrative.

Do you work with organisations?

Yes, corporate programmes, team workshops and talent cohorts are all available. Get in touch to discuss a bespoke proposal.
