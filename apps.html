Leadership Tool

Leadership & Career Framework

The Blueprint for Recognising Your Own Leadership Impact

The Leadership & Career Framework is a structured digital platform built on the Stick Figure Framework, a comprehensive model for understanding and communicating your professional identity.

It brings together every dimension of who you are as a leader into one place: your craft, your leadership evidence, your stories, your habits, and your personal narrative. AI-assisted coaching is built in, helping you refine your thinking and sharpen your pitch at every stage.

Build your career narrative across all 10 leadership dimensions
Store and evidence your STAR stories with AI-powered scoring
Track leadership habits and progress over time
Craft and refine your personal pitch with AI coaching
Mirror reflections to process feedback and build resilience
Eminence tracking for talks, publications and thought leadership
Objectives and actions, goal-setting with accountability built in
What Sets It Apart

Why This Tool Is Unique

Most professionals can describe what they do. Far fewer can articulate who they are as a leader, the specific dimensions they bring, the evidence behind them, and the business case for their next opportunity. The Leadership & Career Framework exists to close that gap.

It is built on the Stick Figure Framework: a structured model that maps every aspect of your professional identity onto a single, coherent picture. Everything connects — your stories feed your spine score, your habits build your evidence, your eminence enriches your narrative, and your pitch is anchored to the specific opportunity you're pursuing.

🛡️

Evidence-led, not assertion-led

Your leadership profile isn't built from self-reported claims, it's built from real, tagged events. Every story and habit contributes to a score that reflects what you've actually demonstrated.

🤖

AI coaching built in

AI doesn't just generate text here. It coaches you through reflection, sharpens your stories, spots patterns in your experience, and helps craft pitches that land.

🔗

Everything connects

Eminence → Story → Habit → Spine → Pitch. Every entry enriches the whole picture. Nothing sits in isolation — the framework is one integrated body.

👤

Recognise your own impact

Many leaders underestimate what they've built. The framework surfaces leadership you're already demonstrating but haven't yet named, making the invisible visible.

🎯

Opportunity-focused

Everything is anchored to a specific context. The platform keeps your development focused on what matters for the role or outcome you're actively working towards.

🧠

Reflective by design

Mirror entries, centring steps, feedback reflection — the platform actively develops the self-awareness that distinguishes truly exceptional leaders.

The Outcome

What You'll Gain

The value of Growth Minded isn't in any single feature — it's in the compounding effect of consistent, structured reflection over time. Here's what users reliably experience when they engage seriously with the platform.

✅

Promotion readiness, whenever it matters

Build a clear, evidence-backed leadership narrative that you can present confidently in interviews, stakeholder conversations, and promotion panels. When the opportunity arrives, you're not starting from scratch.

👤

A living professional identity

Instead of scrambling to update your CV once a year, you maintain a continuously-updated record of who you are and what you've achieved. Your leadership story is always current.

👁️

Greater self-awareness

Structured reflection through Mirror entries, habit tracking, and AI coaching helps you notice patterns in your own leadership that would otherwise remain invisible.

📣

Pitch confidence

From the one-sentence elevator pitch to the full leadership narrative, the platform helps you find authentic, compelling words for who you are and why you lead the way you do.

🔄

A structured development rhythm

Goals, habits, progress logs, and objectives give your development a real cadence, so growth becomes a practice, not a hope. Small, consistent actions compound into significant change.

📈

Visible progress over time

Watching your evidence scores rise as you capture more stories and practise more habits creates genuine motivation. The Anatomy Report lets you see, at a glance, how far you've come.

The honest caveat: Growth Minded rewards consistent use. Users who engage regularly, capturing stories as they happen, logging habit practice, reflecting on feedback promptly, get significantly more value than those who try to catch up once a quarter. Five minutes a week is more valuable than an hour once a month.
